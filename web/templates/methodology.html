🤖 2. Machine Learning Model

Random Forest Regressor

We use an ensemble of 500 decision trees to predict Water Per Capita (WPC).

Why Random Forest?

  • Handles non-linear relationships well
  • Robust to noisy environmental data
  • Provides feature importance rankings

Features Used:

Ward Year Month Season Population Groundwater Lake Storage Rainfall

📏 3. Scoring System

Min-Max Normalization

Score = (Predicted_WPC - Min_WPC) / (Max_WPC - Min_WPC) × 100

Threshold Mapping:

Score Range Label Interpretation
0 – 30 High Scarcity Critical shortage. Immediate action required.
30 – 60 Medium Scarcity Moderate stress. Conservation needed.
60 – 100 Low Scarcity Adequate availability. Sustainable usage.

💧 4. Adaptive Planning Logic

Budget Calculation

Monthly_Budget = Family_Size × 135 L × 30 Days × House_Factor × Scarcity_Factor

House Type Factors:

  • Apartment: ×1.00 (Baseline)
  • Independent House: ×1.15 (+15%)
  • Gated Community: ×1.10 (+10%)
  • Informal Settlement: ×0.90 (-10%)

Task Allocation:

  • Bathing: 32% | Toilet: 28% | Washing: 16%
  • Cooking: 12% | Cleaning: 8% | Gardening: 4%
